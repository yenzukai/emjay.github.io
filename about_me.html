Who am I?

Hello, I'm Martin!

I am a fresh graduate of Partido State University, holding a degree in Information Technology, where I graduated Cum Laude. My background combines both academic excellence and hands-on experience. For five years, I worked as a Network Administrator, managing Ubiquiti, MikroTik, pfSense, and other networking systems. Alongside my studies, I also took on freelance projects as an IT Technician, troubleshooting and maintaining computer systems, CCTV setups, and various IT-related concerns.

For my capstone project, I developed CaptioMirror, a Smart Mirror AI system that integrates IoT, Raspberry Pi, and experimental APIs, showcasing my ability to merge innovation with practical IT solutions. I am now eager to begin my professional career and contribute my skills, adaptability, and passion for technology to a dynamic organization.
